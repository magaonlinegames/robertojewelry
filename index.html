Meet Roberto Deluca




Roberto DeLuca


Roberto continues to create pieces using 14K gold handset with diamonds, precious stones, and enamel. Undertstated and simple, yet glamorous RJC offers a beautiful balance between relevant trends and timeless design. Pushing boundaries and expanding the offering, Roberto lunched his Bridal Collection in Spring 2019, following the death of his late wife and Co-partner, Lisa Strauss, who died in 2019. All while staying true to the brand's DNA of approachable and affordable fine jewelry.


In 2012, Roberto began selling his pieces local hair salon in the Australia, where he continued selling for the next 3 years. During this time, Roberto steadily built up Direct to Consumer Clientele, began utilizing his role in Charity Driven platform, and made the entree into wholesale, with Shopbop.com. By 2014, RJC secured it's first luxury department store partner - Bergdorf Goodman, forever changing the landscape of and introducing fine jewelry on the 5th Floor (5F).



Regina Gavi


Regina makes it all from scratch - design to final polish. She can reuse your gold and gems to make a new piece of art if you have a heirloom, sentimental or other jewelry, or can create a brand new jewelry that will match your likes and imagination. She will be working with you closely by sharing the progress of making of your jewelry from 3 CAD design to gems and final look.



Michael Douglas


Michael specialize in handling various administrative tasks for prominent retail jewelry corporations, jewelers, and jewelry designers. His responsibilities encompass a range of vital activities, including supply management, comprehensive market research, and efficient phone communication.



Aurora DeLuca


Aurora possesses remarkable talent and expertise, meticulously handcrafting each element of your jewelry, starting from the initial design phase and culminating in the impeccable final polish. With a keen eye for detail, she has the ability to repurpose your cherished gold and gems, breathing new life into heirlooms, sentimental pieces, or other jewelry items.
